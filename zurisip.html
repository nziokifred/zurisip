About Us

At Zuri Sip, we believe in delivering refreshment the natural way. We carefully select the finest fruits, sourcing them directly from Kenyan farmers to ensure every ingredient meets our high standards of quality and freshness.

Our juices are crafted to capture the true taste of the fruit — vibrant, pure, and naturally delicious. By working closely with local farmers, we support sustainable agriculture while bringing the freshest flavors from farm to bottle.

At Zuri Sip, every sip tells a story of quality, care, and community. From the orchard to your glass, we are committed to creating a juice experience that is both refreshing and trustworthy.
